Privacy Policy

Effective Date: {{ page.last_modified_at | date: "%B %d, %Y" }}

Elizabeth Rose Florals, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, elizabethroseflorals.com, and use our services. By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you make a purchase through our website. The information collected may include:

  • Personal Identification Information: Your name, email address, billing address, and shipping address.
  • Payment Information: We use Stripe Checkout to process payments. Your payment information is collected and processed by Stripe; we do not store your credit card information on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Order Fulfillment: To process and deliver your orders, including managing payments, fees, and charges.
  • Customer Service: To manage customer relationships, including responding to your inquiries and providing support.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with third parties in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or providing services to you. For example, we use Stripe for payment processing.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or other governmental authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our assets, your information may be transferred to the new owner.

4. Stripe Checkout

We use Stripe Checkout to process payments. Stripe's Privacy Policy governs the processing of your payment information. For more information, please refer to Stripe's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information that we hold. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Effective Date" at the top will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
