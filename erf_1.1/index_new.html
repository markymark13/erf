About Me

Hello there! My name is Elizabeth and I am the founder, owner, and lead designer at Elizabeth Rose Florals.

My desire to join the floral community was spurred by my passion for gardening, the nostalgic, childhood memories of my mom’s garden, and my appreciation for the way flowers emulate emotions. After moving to the beautiful state of Colorado with my husband and doggo, I started my floral endeavor...and here we are!

Flowers bring me joy and I love spreading that feeling to everyone by creating personalized designs that are delicately handcrafted to capture your style and bring your vision to life. I am a big believer in protecting mother nature. I recycle and use sustainable products as much as possible, source locally-grown flowers to the fullest extent, and my designs do not include floral foam, spray paint or dye. Happy flowering!
