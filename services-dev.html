F.A.Qs

Do you offer consultations for wedding and event floral arrangements?

Yes, we provide free personalized consultations to discuss your specific needs, preferences, and vision for your special day. We'll work closely with you to create floral designs that reflect your style and theme.

What types of events do you cater to?

We specialize in a wide range of events including but not limited to weddings, corporate gatherings, private parties, and special occasions.

Can I request custom bouquet designs?

Absolutely! We love creating unique and personalized bouquets tailored to your individual taste and preferences. Whether you prefer classic elegance or contemporary flair, we'll craft a bouquet that perfectly complements your style.

How far in advance should I book your services for my event?

We recommend booking our services as early as possible to secure your date on our calendar. Our weddings and other large scale events usually book out about 6 months to 1 year in advance. We understand that timelines may vary, however, so feel free to reach out to discuss availability at any time.

Do you offer delivery and setup services?

Yes, all full service weddings include delivery and setup to ensure your floral arrangements are delivered safely and set up flawlessly at your venue. Our full service weddings also include takedown. We'll work closely with you and your event coordinator to coordinate delivery times and logistics.

How many weddings do you take on each year?

To ensure that each of our clients receives the utmost attention and personalized service, we limit the number of weddings we take on each year. By focusing on a select number of events, we can dedicate the time and care necessary to bring your floral vision to life with meticulous attention to detail. Due to our commitment to excellence, our calendar tends to fill up quickly, so we encourage you to reach out early to secure your spot and experience the difference of our bespoke floral services.

Do you use local flowers?

We love local florals! We prioritize using locally-sourced, seasonal blooms and eco-conscious practices in our floral designs. We avoid floral foam and strive to minimize waste wherever possible to reduce our environmental impact.
